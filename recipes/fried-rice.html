Fried Rice

Description

Nigerian Fried Rice is a colorful, flavorful rice dish stir-fried with vegetables and seasoned with curry, thyme, and stock. It’s commonly served at parties and pairs well with chicken, fish, or plantain.

Ingredient

  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Pepper
  • Seasoning
  • Chicken stock
  • Liver
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Parboil the rice and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry diced liver/chicken until brown (if using).
  3. Add onions and garlic; stir for 1 minute.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Add curry, thyme, seasoning cubes, and salt.
  6. Pour in chicken stock and add your parboiled rice.
  7. Stir and allow the rice to steam until soft.
  8. Add green pepper last, stir, and serve.

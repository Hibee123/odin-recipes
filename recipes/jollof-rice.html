Jollof Rice

Description

A West African classic rice dish cooked with tomatoes, peppers, and spices.

Ingredient

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Seasoning
  • Chicken stock

Steps

  1. Blend the peppers
  2. Fry the tomatoes
  3. Add the peppers
  4. Fry till the oil float over the pepper sauce
  5. Add Seasoning
  6. Add chicken stock
  7. Add your rice
  8. Cook until tender

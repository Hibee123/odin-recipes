Egusi Soup

Description

Egusi Soup is a rich West African melon seed–based soup cooked with vegetables, palm oil, and meat or fish. It is thick, hearty, and typically enjoyed with pounded yam, eba, fufu, or semo.

Ingredient

  • Egusi
  • Ugu
  • Pepper
  • Seasoning
  • Meat stock
  • Meat
  • Palm oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Season and boil your meat until tender. Keep the stock.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry diced liver/chicken until brown (if using).
  3. Fry for 3–5 minutes.
  4. Add ground egusi and fry gently (or add as a lump “caking” method).
  5. Add meat stock gradually and stir until thick.
  6. Add cooked meat, crayfish, seasoning cubes, and salt.
  7. Allow to cook for 10–15 minutes until the egusi is fluffy.
  8. Add vegetables last and stir.
  9. Cook for an additional 3 minutes and serve.
